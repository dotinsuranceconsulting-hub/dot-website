FAQ

Common questions about insurtech operations consulting

What is insurtech operations consulting?+

Insurtech operations consulting helps insurance startups build and mature the internal infrastructure they need to scale — covering digital systems, operational processes, and technology. Unlike traditional consultants who deliver recommendations, an insurtech operations consultant like DOT executes the work end-to-end.

What types of insurance startups do you work with?+

DOT Insurtech Solutions works with early to growth-stage insurtech startups that need to mature their internal operations. This includes MGAs, insurtechs, insurance brokerages, and captive programs that are scaling fast and need operational infrastructure to match.

How is DOT different from a traditional operations consultant?+

Most consultants advise. DOT executes. Every service offered is something that has been personally done before at an insurance startup — from implementing a Policy Administration System to building out an HR function from scratch. There are no junior staff, no hand-offs, and no strategy decks without delivery.

What does a typical engagement look like?+

Engagements are project-based and scoped around specific initiatives — such as implementing a new system, building out a department process, or supporting a leadership team through a period of rapid growth. Start with a free 30-minute consultation to discuss your biggest operational challenges.

Do you work remotely or on-site?+

DOT Insurtech Solutions works remotely with insurance startups across the United States. Remote-first engagements keep costs low while maintaining the same level of hands-on execution and responsiveness you would expect from an in-house hire.
